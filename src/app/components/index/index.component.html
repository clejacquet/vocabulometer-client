About the app

Vocabulometer aims at helping English learners by offering a platform for vocabulary learning and training, using an eye-tracker device. Vocabulometer works with the Tobii EyeX and the Tobii 4C.

This project has been developed by a student from the Intelligent Media Processing laboratory, in the Osaka Prefecture University. This website has been created for research purpose, as part of exploring how to use new technology devices such as eye-trackers to enhance people's lives.

Eye gaze analysis algorithms and some texts are taken from a prototype built by University of Bordeaux students.

Welcome to Vocabulometer!

Hello! This is your first time in Vocabulometer, so we need to know a bit more about you before letting you use our learning services.

This platform uses your English vocabulary to recommend texts that correspond to your level. Yet as you are a new user we have no information about your vocabulary!

Vocabulometer offers you two options to solve this issue. Either solve a quiz that will determine your level, or give your CEFR level or TOEIC / TOEFL / IELTS score if you passed any of these tests.

Please select one of the two options.
